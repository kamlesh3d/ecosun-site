📚 DCR Certificate — Puri Jankari

❓ DCR Kya Hai?

  • Domestic Content Requirement
  • Solar cells & modules India mein bane hon
  • Government subsidy ke liye mandatory
  • NISE portal se verify hota hai
  • Har module ka unique serial number hota hai

✅ DCR Kyun Zaroori Hai?

  • PM Surya Ghar subsidy ke liye
  • PM KUSUM scheme ke liye
  • Sarkar tender ke liye
  • Made in India initiative
  • June 2025 se mandatory

📋 DCR Process Steps

  1. DCR Portal visit karein (NISE)
  2. Solar module serial number enter karein
  3. Verify DCR compliance status
  4. Certificate download karein
  5. DISCOM ko submit karein
